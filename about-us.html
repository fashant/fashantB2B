    About Us

    Make fashion shopping
    in Malls
    Convenient, Personal and Exciting.

    At Fashant, we see the world differently from others! We aren’t your typical e- commerce website. We offer a unique experience: One that makes your shopping convenient without actually changing the way you shop.

    With the rise of E-commerce and delivery sites, online shopping has been made easier than ever. But, what about us paranoids who hate online shopping and need to try out clothes before we buy them? What about us shopaholics whose entertainment is hunting out the best options in malls?

    That’s why at Fashant we work very hard to make shopping better for the mall lovers. We strive to create a mall shopping experience that is convenient, fun and exciting.

    Brand Story

    Fashant is the brainchild of our Founder, Saigokul Kannan. We’ve all had one of those unfortunate shopping days, when we left home excited to shop but returned back with an almost empty shopping bag and a sad face. The items we loved weren’t available in our size, or we were left unsatisfied with the options we found. All the more, we simply didn’t have the time or energy to scan the infinite number of brands and options. It was one such experience that got Saigokul, thinking about a potential solution to the problem that plagues millions of shoppers. He was quick to notice the differences in the shopping culture between the UAE and other countries. He built Fashant because he believes that UAE residents deserve a convenient way to shop for fashion in malls. That’s why at Fashant we empower customers with information to help quench their thirst for fashion. Fashant isn’t here to change the way you shop. It’s here to help you shop smarter!
