    Questions about Fashant Marketplace

    What is Fashant?

    Fashant is a website/app that provides fashion lovers in the region a convenient mall shopping experience. We have a marketplace, blogs and forums. Marketplace: Provides information on fashion products available in malls for your favorite brands.
    Blogs: Provides fun and exciting content on fashion to keep you engaged.
    Forum: Ask/Answer questions and engage with the fashion community.

    How is Fashant different from Namshi, Souq, 6thStreet and all other Online Shopping sites?

    We aren’t an E-commerce website trying to sell our products. We are an information provider that helps find what you’re looking for. Think of Fashant like Google or Zomato, but specifically for Fashion. We provide information about fashion products available in malls. We also have blogs and forums to help quench your thirst for fashion.

    Why should I use Fashant?

    Fashant is made for fashion fanatics who love shopping in malls. We make your shopping experience 10x more convenient! You no more need to run around the mall, dig through racks and bother store assistants to find what you’re looking for. You can Window Shop what’s in store (from our participating brands), add items to your wishlist, and then visit stores once you have a better idea of what you want. Our blogs will be sure to keep you updated on the latest and greatest in fashion. And our forum is a growing platform of fashion discussions.

    Questions about Fashant

    How does the Fashant Marketplace Work?

    We display what fashion items are available in-store for our participating brands. It’s like scanning the menu of a restaurant on Zomato, but better! At Fashant, you can window shop what’s available, check price and size availability ahead of time. You can add products you like to your wishlist, and even reserve online and try in store.

    Are the brands displayed in Fashant real/authentic?

    Yes! We work with 100% authentic brands, and brands are required to provide us necessary documents before we display their products in our portal. We believe in providing reliable customer service! If you visit stores and find that the products are not authentic, please do write to us, and we will make sure to have these items delisted from our portal.

    What is the ‘Check Availability’ feature?

    Have you had a situation where you visited a store, found an item you liked, only to realize that it wasn’t available in your size? Yes of course, we all have! What a waste of time right? That’s why we created the check availability feature. When you see an item online you like, you can then check its availability in-store for your size (before you visit the store). This allows you to make your shopping experience efficient.

    What is the ‘Reserve Online and Try in Store’ feature?

    Once you find an item you like online, you can reserve this item to try in store (no fee). Yes! We understand that you want to try on and feel an item before you purchase it. That’s why we introduced this feature. You can choose items you like, and then reserve these items to come try them out in-store. You have to select a time slot (same day only), and hit reserve. You will be notified once the items are reserved for you. PS: This feature will not work for items that are the last piece.

    Why can’t I order items for delivery?

    This isn’t an online delivery website. We are made for mall shoppers who are looking to make their experience convenient. We might later bring in delivery, but for now, we help you find things in malls.

    Why can’t I buy through Fashant?

    Fashant isn’t an E-commerce website. We are an information provider. We help you find what you’re looking for, and make your experience better.

    What are Rewards points?

    On every purchase (at a participating store) you get reward points equal to the amount of the purchase. All you need to do is let the shopkeeper know that you are a Fashant User.

    How do I use my Reward points?

    100 reward points = 1 AED. You can use these reward points for cash back vouchers (minimum 20AED), at any of our participating stores. We will soon be coming up with more exciting ways for you to redeem your rewards.

    Questions about Fashant Blogs

    What are Fashant Blogs?

    Looking for the latest trends in fashion? Then Fashant blogs are for you! We provide engaging content to help you stay up-to-date with the latest fashion trends. We also provide useful tips, advice and the occasional memes.

    How do you pick the blog topics?

    We have a panel that is consistently hunting for what’s trending in the industry. We also take common questions/concerns from our forum and turn them into blogs.

    How often do new blogs appear?

    We look to publish one almost every other day. Our goal currently is to publish around 3 a week.

    How do I get notified when a new blog is released?

    You can sign up for the Fashant Newsletter. In the newsletter we provide information on all things Fashant. Or, once you’ve signed in to your Fashant account, you can also choose to get notified on the blog page. Whatever suits you best!

    Can I suggest blog topics?

    Absolutely! Please feel free to email us or comment in our forum/blogs to let us know what topics you would like to see. After all, it’s YOU we write the blogs for!

    Can I write blogs?

    Yes, you absolutely can! Please email us any submissions on contact@fashant.com. We will review your submission, and if it is selected, we will publish it and give you credit for the work. You also will be honored in our blog section, if you continue to write a lot.

    Questions about Fashant Forum

    What is the Fashant Forum?

    Fashant Forum is an open discussion platform where you can post questions, discussion topics etc. on fashion. This allows you to engage with the fashion community.

    How do I post questions?

    Once you click the two main sub-topics (Men/Women) you should find a box where you can type your question/discussion topic.

    What questions can I ask?

    You can ask any question as long it is loosely related to fashion. Be your best judge and keep it friendly.

    How do I answer questions?

    You need to be Signed-In to answer. If you’re not, please create your Fashant Account. It’s super quick. Shouldn’t take you more than 60 seconds! Once you’re Signed-In, you will notice a box under each question that allows you to type in a response.
